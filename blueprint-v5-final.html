Privacy
How your data is stored

All data — habits, journals, CBT records, XP — is stored only on this device in localStorage. Nothing is ever sent to any server. There is no account, no cloud sync, no tracking. Your most sensitive entries (CBT, journal) never leave your browser. Use Export Backup regularly to keep a safe copy.
